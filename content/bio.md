Pierce Purselley is a writer and comedian based in Los Angeles. He is the Showrunner of Mortified Los Angeles — a live show, Netflix documentary, and television series in which adults share their most embarrassing childhood diaries, letters, and songs on stage in front of strangers.

<br>

He writes across animation and live action, with original projects in adult animation, young adult series, and limited drama. He is also the creator of THE SWARMS, an ongoing webcomic.

<br>

He is based in Los Angeles.
